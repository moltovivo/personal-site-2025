JPMorgan Bio

Hi, I'm Aaron Mulvihill. I work as a Global Alternatives Strategist, which means I develop insights into markets and the economy, to help investors make more informed decisions. My focus is alternative investments, which is anything other than stocks & bonds. That can include real assets, like data centers, real estate, and the infrastructure behind the power grid. I also cover private equity, private credit, hedge funds and other assets.

My background is in operations, finance and M&A across the infrastructure space. I spent 12 years with CRH, the world's largest diversified building solutions provider. I managed factories, held various financial leadership roles, and worked on M&A to grow the business. That work allowed me to spend time in many countries, especially in Eastern Europe, the Netherlands and Australia and throughout the US.

I grew up in Ireland but now live in New York. I have enjoyed tech and coding all my life, and am often working on projects for my own enjoyment, or to benefit my community.
